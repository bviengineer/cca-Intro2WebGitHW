Quality Time

Have you ever felt overjoyed when, important to, loved by, a person who took time out to spend with you? Maybe Quality Time is your love language! Making a priority to spend time with and with little to no interruptions, a loved-one whose love language is quality time will emotionally fill that person with bounds of varying emotions including joy, renewed confidence, hope, to name a few.
