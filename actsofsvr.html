The Five Love Languages

Acts of Service

What's come to mind when you hear the phrase "Acts of Service?" Acts of Servce, in the context of a love language, is purposely doing things the other person would like or take notice of and greatly appreciate, such as cooking or cleaning on their behalf, serving them a drink. etcetera.
