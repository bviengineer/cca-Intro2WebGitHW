Acts of Service

What comes to mind when you hear the phrase "Acts of Service?" Acts of Servce, in the context of a love language, is purposely doing things the other person would like or take notice of and greatly appreciate, such as taking care of errands, cooking or cleaning on their behalf, serving them a drink. etcetera.
